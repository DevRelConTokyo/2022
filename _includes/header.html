DevRelCon Yokohama 2023:

DevRelCon Yokohama is an English language conference bringing the developer relations community back together in person for two days of sharing, learning, and communing.

If you have a talk in mind, we’d love to hear from you. We’re open to talks on any developer relations, community, and developer marketing topics. Themes we’re especially interested in are developer relations for employer branding and developer relations in a recession. The CfP closing date is December 31th, 2022.

DevRelCon Yokohama 2023 is brought to you by MOONGIFT and DevRel Meetup in Tokyo.

Everyone participating in DevRelCon agrees to abide by our code of conduct.
